# 🚲 Southern Spokes Bike Share Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using **SQL** and **Power BI** to analyze the performance of a bike-sharing business.

The goal was to transform raw operational data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, interactive dashboards, and strategic recommendations.

The dashboard focuses on:

- Revenue Analysis
- Profit Analysis
- Rider Growth
- Customer Demographics
- Seasonal Performance
- Hourly Sales Trends
- Pricing Analysis

  ## 🎯 Business Problem

The objective of this project was to analyze two years of bike-sharing data to understand business performance and answer key strategic questions, including:

- How has revenue and profitability changed over time?
- Which customer segments contribute the most value?
- What are the peak demand periods?
- How does seasonality influence rider activity?
- Is the current pricing strategy supporting business growth?

- ## 📈 Key Insights

- Generated **$15.18M revenue** and **$10.45M profit** over two years, showing strong business performance.  
- **80%+ of customers are registered riders**, indicating strong retention and loyalty.  
- Revenue growth is driven mainly by **higher pricing rather than increased ride volume**.  
- Demand peaks during **morning and evening commute hours**, confirming commuter-driven usage.  
- **Season 3 delivered the highest revenue**, highlighting clear seasonal impact.  

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Apply **dynamic pricing during peak hours** to maximise revenue.  
- Focus on **high-value segments (registered riders & commuters)**.  
- Strengthen **customer retention through loyalty programs and targeted offers**.  
- Use **seasonal + hourly demand patterns** for better planning and pricing strategy.  
- Continuously monitor **profitability, demand, and customer behaviour** for optimisation.
